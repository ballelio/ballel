The Book of Love

Book Design. The Book of Love is a thorough analysis of relationships between men and women, and delves deep into the psychology of men and women. The original manuscript is called "Hints for Lovers", and can be found at gutenberg.org.

This book was selected Best in Category in Student Cover, and First Place in Student Interior at the 58th Annual New England Book Show, 2015.

"And love, is not the easy thing..."

When I came across this manuscript, I thought I had a piece with a lot of potential for development with the added challenge of having romantic love as the subject matter. With such an ubiquitous and subjective concept, my intention was to evoke the most basic qualities associated with love by the use of warm colors and organic shapes in a minimalistic approach, while staying away from heart shapes and overused imagery. For the format, the way in which the book was written allowed me to work with an unusual size of 4.5" x 9" and use diverse typographical variations.
