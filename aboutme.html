About Me

My name is Liliana Ballesteros and I’m a problem-solver who enjoys learning new things, and wants to grow as a Project Manager.


I’m a graphic design by trade with years of production and project management experience in a marketing and communications environment. One day, I wanted to build an online portfolio, and all I had was basic HTML and CSS. Needless to say, I needed more than that, and over the past few years—in my free time—I’ve been teaching myself different front-end languages and frameworks, and WordPress devepment seeing how digital products come to life.


Along came UX, information architecture, digital marketing, content strategy, accessibility, the conferences, the meetups, the code challenges, the hackathon… the list goes on, but it wasn’t until (not knowing what I didn’t know) I came across a course in Digital Project Management, that I understood this is where I’m meant to be. Project Management is where my interests intersect; design, communication, technology, and of course, making it all happen.
