The Andes and the Amazon eBook

Ebook development and cover design.

This is a historic, descriptive document about the journey of James Orton across South America in the 1800s. Originally published in 1869 by Harper and Brothers. Manuscript can be found at gutenberg.org.

This ebook was selected Best in Category in Student Ebook at the 58th Annual New England Book Show, 2015. Available for download

South America in its purest state

After becoming familiar with the content, I was inspired by the subject matter and the time in which the book was written. For the cover I chose a picture taken from Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta in the north coast of Colombia and gave it a vintage feel. The cover was the starting point and from there, the colors for the body, headings, tables, and the other elements were chosen. Originally, the manuscript had unedited black and white (scanned) illustrations that were enhanced by adding color to some of their elements, while preserving the original quality of the drawings.
